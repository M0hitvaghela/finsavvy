Frequently Asked Questions

Is FinSavvy really free?

Yes! Our Basic plan is 100% free—no credit card required. You only pay if you choose to upgrade for Pro features.

Can I access FinSavvy on multiple devices?

Yes! You can log in to your FinSavvy account from any device with internet access, and your data will stay synchronized.

How secure is my data?

We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption and secure backups. Your data is yours alone.

What if I forget my password?

No worries! Use the “Forgot Password” link on the login page to reset your password securely via email.

Can I sync FinSavvy with my bank account?

No, currently FinSavvy does not support direct bank account syncing. However, you can manually input transactions and track your expenses easily.
